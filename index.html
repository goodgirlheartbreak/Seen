Before We Begin
Hear this
first.

You are not alone.

You are not crazy.

Take a breath. This is your safe space to make sense of a situation that makes absolutely no sense.

Your world has come crashing down. Your spouse cheated emotionally or physically over an extended period of time. He may have had an outside child because of it.

You are not okay. And you do not have to be.

Your emotions are valid. Every single one of them. The rage. The grief. The confusion. The days you can barely get out of bed and the days you feel fine and then feel guilty for feeling fine.

God is big enough to handle your emotions. The good, the bad, and the ugly. Bring it all.

Choosing to stay today does not always mean choosing to stay tomorrow. And that is okay. Your healing is the priority.
